Is this really legal?

Under the 2018 Farm Bill, hemp products are federally legal as long as they contain less than 0.3% Delta-9 THC by dry weight.

Every Farmer Joe product is tested by independent labs. Certificates of Analysis (COAs) are available right on our product pages and linked via QR codes on our packaging. No detours, no filler websites — just direct access to real lab results.

Every product is carefully crafted and rigorously tested to ensure it meets this strict legal requirement, so you can enjoy with confidence and peace of mind. Trust is non-negotiable for us and we share the test results to prove it!

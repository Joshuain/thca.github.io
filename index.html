Is this really legal?

Under the 2018 Farm Bill, hemp products are federally legal as long as they contain less than 0.3% Delta-9 THC by dry weight.

Every Pine Park product is tested by independent labs. Certificates of Analysis (COAs) are available right on our product pages and linked via QR codes on our packaging.
